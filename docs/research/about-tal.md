## Why this site exists

- **Primary goal: community standing** in the (Israeli and wider) dev
  community. Not primarily a job board asset or client funnel — the writing,
  translations, and craft are the point, and identity decisions should weight
  accordingly.
- The portfolio is itself a proof of capability: the infrastructure behind it
  is part of the work being shown, not just a delivery mechanism. Note the
  continuity: Tal's Amdocs work already included design systems and
  AI-augmented development — this site extends both threads in public.
