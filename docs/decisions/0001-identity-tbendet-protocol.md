## Context
The portfolio needs an identity that reads as a developer's, not a template's.

## Decision
`T://bendet` — a personal protocol/namespace. `T` is the scheme, `bendet` is the
payload. Used as logo, hero centrepiece, and the eyebrow-label prefix throughout
(`T://bendet · about`, etc.). The `://` carries a subtle glow treatment.

## Consequences
Every page carries the eyebrow pattern. The mark must survive whatever Mission 1
decides about Greek mythology — M1 may recontextualize it but replacing it
requires superseding this ADR.

## Alternatives rejected
Plain wordmark; handle-style branding (@bendet). Both generic.
