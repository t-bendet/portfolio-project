## Decision
Tal's face (bearded, glasses) from the wedding-invitation caricature drawn by
Tal's wife. Placement: About page beside the bio (totaltypescript.com wizard
pattern) and favicon (tight crop, reads at 32px).

## Rationale
A hand-drawn human face against monospace precision is the most distinctive
element on the site and cannot be replicated by anyone else.
