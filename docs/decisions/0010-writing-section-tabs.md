## Decision (as originally made)
One /writing section, tab switcher: Original (English posts) and Translated
(English articles translated to Hebrew for the Israeli dev community, with
original-author credit). Post count badges on tabs.

## Why reopened
Tal proposed translated articles as a subsection inside Articles instead.
Mission 4 decides the content model and navigation.

## Preserved requirement
Whatever the structure, translated posts render dir="rtl" and credit the
original article and author. See ADR 0011.
